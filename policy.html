Policy & Compliance Statement

From Stephen Kioko Mwania, Director & CEO

"We aim at consistently enhancing the quality of our products while ensuring the safety of both individuals and the environment. We commit and are dedicated to safeguarding and continuously enhancing the quality, environment, health and occupational safety of our personnel at all times."

Our Commitments

At Lekisa Trading Limited, we are dedicated to upholding the highest standards in all our operations. Our policy commitments include:

Safeguard Quality & Safety

Continuously enhance the quality, environment, health and occupational safety of our personnel at all times.

Eliminate Non-Conformances

Identify and eliminate quality non-conformances through rigorous quality control processes.

Performance Framework

Establish a framework for defining quality and performance goals, and regularly evaluate and enhance processes, services and product quality.

Customer Satisfaction

Surpass customer requirements and ensure top-notch satisfaction to all customers.

Regulatory Compliance

Adhere to all relevant environmental and safety laws, values and regulations as we believe it is essential for conducting business responsibly.

Environmental Responsibility

Reduce our environmental footprint by preventing pollution and minimizing waste.

Employee Participation

Ensure employees actively participate in and are consulted on matters concerning occupational health and safety.

Transparent Communication

Maintain transparent communication with stakeholders to ensure they understand our policies, standards programs and performance.

Our Ongoing Commitment

This policy will undergo regular reviews to ensure its continued alignment with regulations, standards and applicable laws and regulations in all our operational locations.

Regular Reviews

We continuously evaluate our policies to ensure compliance with changing regulations.

Performance Monitoring

We track our performance against established benchmarks and goals.

Continuous Improvement

We invest in training and development to enhance our practices continually.

Contact us to learn more about our commitment to quality, safety, and environmental responsibility.
